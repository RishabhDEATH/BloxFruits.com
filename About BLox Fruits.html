What is Blox fruits?

Blox Fruits is a popular online multiplayer game on the Roblox platform, offering players an immersive experience in a vibrant world inspired by the anime series "One Piece."
In this game, players embark on adventures, explore islands, battle enemies, and discover legendary fruits granting unique powers.
Each fruit confers distinct abilities, encouraging strategic gameplay and fostering a sense of progression.
